Hansel and Gretel

Hansel and Gretel were brother and sister who lived near a forest.
One day, they were left in the woods and became lost.
As they wandered, they found a house made of candy and sweets.
Hungry, they began to eat it.
Suddenly, an old woman came out and invited them inside.
She was actually a witch who planned to trap them.
The witch locked Hansel in a cage and made Gretel do chores.
But Gretel was clever. One day, she tricked the witch and pushed her into the oven.
The children escaped, found their way home, and were reunited with their family, never going hungry again.
